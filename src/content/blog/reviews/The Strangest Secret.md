First recorded as a spoken word in 1956, for which is was the first to turn Gold by selling over a million copies.

**Earl Nightingale** was a American radio host and author. Nightingale was in the marine corps during world war 2 and was present during pearl harbour and one of the few to walk away from the ship he was on. After his military career Nightingale began work in the radio industry which lead to recording multiple spoken records. Later he was inducted into the National Speakers Association and National Association of Broadcasters National Hall of Fame. 

# Overview
From the outside looking in this book may seem like another empty promise on easy money or some redundant ethical guidebook based on the limited impressions of an entrepreneurial author, but at a second glance, this book offers a fundamental psychological truism.

I'll save you the read;

> "*We become what we think about*"


This is the strangest secret. That by thinking about something in a cheerful, relaxed, positive way the goal achieves itself almost miraculously, without any hardened effort or swallowing of nails, we can make even the most difficult tasks seem simple if we would just wish them consistently and exclusively. The book is littered with wonderful quotes and proverbs I will not waste time repeating them as this itself takes the majority of the book.

## Best Definition of Success
What is success? an important question if we want to be successful. The book posits something I believe to be very profound.

> *"success is the progressive realisation of a worthy ideal"*

This is something worth repeating, *"success is the progressive realisation of a worthy ideal"*, in other words success is the pursuit of a well defined and worthy goal. Success might be the baker who produces quality bread for his community, it might be the farmer who prospers throughout another harvest, it might be the school teacher who is teaching because that is what they want to do.
Lets consider in parts the anatomy of this definition.
1. **well defined ideal**
	you want to a lot of money, well lets get specific how much is a lot? get to the core of what you want, how much? how far? how long? what would the achievement of your goal look like, make it verifiable.
2.  **worthy**
	What makes an ideal worthy: an ideal is worthy when it challenging yet realistic. Although unmentioned I would add an ethical necessity, it has to help us or others, a goal who's aim hurts or destroys rather than help and create is unworthy.
3. **progressive realisation**
	Progressive means moving towards, and realisation means acceptance - we want to moving towards succeeding in this ideal. Although we might have a clear defined idea of our goal, but how do we get there? The aim of success is that we are moving towards understanding this ideal and how to get there.

## We become what we think about
We become what we think about all day long, we can be nothing else. As man is the sum total of the conditions and impressions laid upon him, so may he act through this by the usage of clear positive visualisation. What relation do we actual have to the real word? we may sense it, empirically we may listen and feel and touch but the real conceptive ability all happens when we think of it retrospectively, this is where true lessons are learned and context is achieved. So by focusing our attention on a goal, with the right intentions, we can guide our sub-conscious into giving us the answers we need. It is similar to the ideas represented in the book Pycho-Cybernetics by Maxwell Maltz: the brain and sub conscious are a chaotic machine, but by feeding it the correct mental impressions through active thought we can guide this machine. The creative inner workings are a natural end result of guiding ourselves in a care free way towards our goals.

## 30 day challenge
Nightingale finally urges us to take into action, and to see the results yourself.
1. write down a clearly defined goal on a piece of paper and carry it everywhere, look at it constantly and think about your goal is a relaxed, cheerful way.
2. Stop thinking about what you fear, every time a fear or doubt comes up in your mind replace it with the positive image of your worthwhile goal.
3. success is in direct proportion to our service, Be of service … build … work … dream … create, don't worry about money, or fruits, concern yourself with the labour of helping and creating. 
Do this for 30 days.
