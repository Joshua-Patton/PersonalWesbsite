# only when man is silent is he free
We don't learn anything when we speak, only when we listen.
Firstly to others, to what they are really saying and meaning, to how and what they wish to communicate in truth or express in feeling. We may ask questions to further this, but what someone chooses to talk about unprompted often is half the story.
Secondly when we listen to ourselves, when we starting feeling the weight of ourselves pushing against the floor or seat, the sun on our back, the sounds, tastes and feelings that are in this moment, that your body and heart are trying so desperate to communicate of which we choose to ignore, because we are too caught up listening to our own mouths, again which is the manifestation of ego.
Thirdly when we start listening to universe, we start to realise it was speaking to us all along, not by means of patterns in the night sky, or numerology in the signposts. But if we bare witness to its properties, of symmetry, flux and fragmentation- we find a never-ending sea of new.

This by the way is the trinity which is everything, others, the self, and the universe, which are all connected - the self perceives, others confirm and the universe commands.

When we lose ego, we learn - for there is no clouding from conception, we learn to accept the creative gifts from the muse and the love from the universe. To speak is akin to begging for these gifts, but listening is accepting and heeding them when they appear, it is the wisdom of patience but also the virtue of faith for there can be no expectation only a silent surrender with our head bowed down in pure humility.

Mostly what I mean when i say 'speaking' has nothing to do with the literal vocal organ, but the mere presence of control that our ego craves, once we let go, when we begin to be silent, when we quite all that loudness, once we shut up - then we may begin to listen and maybe also if we are lucky - we may learn something.
